### How do I activate this integration?

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Log in to your Onvo account, navigate to the Integrations page, and click on
    `Connect` next to the Wafeq card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    You will be shown a modal by Rootfi that shows you a list of permissions.
    Clicking accept redirects you to a page where you can authenticate Wafeq
    with Onvo AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    After authentication, return to Onvo's Data sources page and click on `New
    data source`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Provide a name for the data source, an optional description, and select
    Wafeq as the type. Then click on `Connect`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fifth Step">
    On the appearing page, specify the resource and click on `Save changes` to
    see your data descriptions and sample data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![title](/assets/integrations/wafeq-1.png)

### What can I do with this integration?

You can import accounts data from Wafeq as data sources on Onvo. The integration ensures that any updates in your Wafeq module are reflected in real-time on your dashboards.

Build interactive dashboards using your accounts data, effortlessly creating widgets based on your visualization preferences. Ask questions in natural language to explore your accounts data, and perform data pre-processing before visualization. Integrating dashboards into your products with the SDK is also supported.

![title](/assets/integrations/common-dashboard.png)

### Config parameters

<ParamField path="company_id" type="number">
  The company id from Rootfi
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="resource" type="string">
  This would be the resource you want to fetch. It can be one of the following:
  `accounts`, `balance_sheet`, `bank_accounts`, `bank_transactions`,
  `bill_credit_notes`, `bill_payments`, `bills`, `cash_flow_statement`,
  `contacts`, `currencies`, `documents`, `expenses`, `income_statement`,
  `invoice_credit_notes`, `invoice_payments`, `invoices`, `items`,
  `journal_entries`, `journal_lines`, `line_items`, `purchase_orders`,
  `sales_orders`, `tax_rates`
</ParamField>
